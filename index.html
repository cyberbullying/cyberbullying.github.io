Cyber Bullying
By Federico D'Agata

Cyberbullying is a form of bullying that is done through the medium of technology. It can occur through texting and apps. It is most common on the internet on social media sites and forums. Cyberbullying is usually achieved by sending hateful, rude or untrue posts, images or comments regarding a targeted individual/group.
